# Rally Epic E5102 — Deal qualification agent

*Archetype: greenfield. ObjectVersion 7 · LastUpdate 2026-06-20.*

## Description
a deal-qualification agent that scores inbound sales opportunities. enrich the lead from CRM and firmographic sources, then score fit and intent. route qualified deals to an account executive. reads CRM opportunities and writes qualification scores back to CRM.

## Acceptance Criteria
- qualification must complete within 2 minutes at the 95th percentile

## Non-Functional Requirements
- all customer contact data is encrypted at rest and in transit
